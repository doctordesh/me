What about me?

So, who am I? My name is Emil and I'm a developer, gamer, musician and a lot of other things, but foremost a developer. I live and breath code and I love the craftsmanship that programming is. Beautiful code is like art, magnificent and wonderful art! I love to try out new technologies and keep up with the latest practices, pushing the boundaries that is my own knowledge.

As a person, I really like to develop my mind, to discuss ideas about the world and life. I find it interesting to meet new people because they always have a perspective that I don't.

When I'm not programming I go for a run or lift heavy iron things from the floor, cook a good meal or enjoy a movie. I read books and I watch Youtube-series about physics, maths and astronomy.

And coffee, I really like coffee!
